Skill Otters: Building Community Rafts
Skill Otters is a student-led skill share organization dedicated to enriching our local community by connecting people through the sharing of skills. We noticed that many people in our area have hobbies and talents in common, but rarely speak to each other or collaborate. Inspired by otters—community creatures who share and play together throughout their lives—we want to create 'Rafts' that strengthen our community and, one day, communities around the world.
Our mission is to provide a platform where members can share what they know, learn from others, and build lasting friendships. We believe that everyone has something valuable to offer, and by coming together, we can make our community stronger, more vibrant, and more connected. The funds raised will go directly toward materials, event space, and promotions, helping us reach not just our local neighborhood but people across the metro Atlanta area who are looking for a Raft to join.
As many teenagers and adults are realizing this summer, it's hot, there's nothing to do but be on your phone all day, and everything fun costs money. We want to change that. We want to bring community and fun, safe spaces back for everyone to enjoy. Your support will help us create opportunities for learning, sharing, and connection—making our community a place where everyone feels welcome and inspired.
